REAL-TIME MEDIA TRANSACTIONS

Events don't wait, neither should you. You see it, you ask for it, you get it. Everything in real-time.

LARGE FILES, VIDEO, & METADATA

Technology problems solved. Any format, any file, with full metadata. Seamless transactions delivered securely.

REQUEST EXCLUSIVES & ORIGINALS

Asking for the tweet is just a part - you can also ask for never-before-seen content in any transaction.

EASY LICENSING & TERMS

With a few clicks you can select from a default list of licensing agreements or upload your own.

SECURE ONLINE PAYMENTS

Direct payments, invoicing, reporting, and even system integration - everything to make transactions seamless.

TEAMS & MULTIPLE TWITTER ACCOUNTS

Designed with newsrooms in mind. Multiple users & twitter accounts for your outgoing requests.

POWERFUL EDITORIAL DASHBOARD

All requests and transactions organized, searchable, and available to your team in the editorial dashboard.

EXTENSIVE API & ENTERPRISE INTEGRATION

Available custom integration with any CMS, DAM, MAM, accounting & rights-management systems.
